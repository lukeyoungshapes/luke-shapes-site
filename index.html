Why choose LY surfboards

At LY Surfboards we appreciate that no two surfers are the same, that's why we have such a diverse range of models to cater for any style or ability — from beginners boards right through to big guns and tow boards. Even though we use models we are still a 'custom surfboards' business. We tailor every board to each individual customers requirements — also if a customer has an idea of their own we're more than happy to build it for them. With the combination of only the most premium quality materials, second to none craftsmanship and the best possible customer care, we strive for the ultimate service.
