Prodium

high performance shortboard replacing the dogtool model for experienced through to pro surfers. A continuous nose to tail rocker curve allows for tight, vertical pocket surfing in good quality waves. The Prodium has a deep single mid, to sunk double concave through the fin cluster adding a little more drive and control, coupled with a low mid rail through to foiled aft rail for grip and drive.
